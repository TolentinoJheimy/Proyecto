Why was ForestGuard created?

The Problem

Forest Fires

Every year, thousands of hectares of forest are consumed by fires that destroy flora, fauna, and natural resources.

Environmental Impact

The loss of biodiversity and the increase in polluting emissions directly affect the balance of the ecosystem.

Social Impact

Communities, firefighters, and families nearby face constant risks during each fire season.

Our mission

ForestGuard Objectives

ForestGuard seeks to prevent forest fires through an intelligent monitoring system that allows detecting risk conditions before the fire spreads.

Detect potential fires at an early stage.
Reduce environmental impact and protect forests.
Provide useful information to act quickly.
Promote the use of IoT technologies in environmental protection.

System process

How does ForestGuard work?

Sensors

Capture temperature, humidity, and smoke presence.

ESP32

Processes the data obtained by the sensors.

Wi-Fi

Sends the information to the server via the Internet.

Server

Flask receives and organizes the system data.

Alert

The user receives a warning when there is a risk of fire.
